About PriceSense

PriceSense is a smart e-commerce analytics platform designed to help sellers and businesses predict product prices with AI-powered accuracy. Our mission is to empower users with real-time market insights, anomaly detection, and strategic recommendations for optimal pricing decisions.

Our platform features a modern dashboard, secure authentication, and interactive tools for analyzing product and market data. Whether you're a new seller or an established brand, PriceSense provides the analytics you need to stay competitive in today's fast-moving e-commerce landscape.

Developed by a team passionate about data science and e-commerce, PriceSense combines technology and industry expertise to deliver actionable insights and drive business growth.
